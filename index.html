Retail Analysis & Buying

Building a six-month buying plan for Five Below's Beauty & Style department.

Type

Retail Health Analysis

Market

NYC, 51 Locations

Category

Beauty & Style

Tools

Retail Trend Research, Category Analysis

Process

I compared Five Below against Dollar General and Dollar Tree, then identified a $0.40 per unit sourcing savings between Singapore and China. Not a rounding error at Five Below's volume. I built a subcategory allocation plan with specific percentage splits based on trend data and sales performance.

Takeaway

Retail decisions that look like taste or intuition usually have data behind them.
